In the last 6 years National Institute of Cardiovascular Diseases (NICVD) has transformed into one of the largest and best cardiac centre in the world. In 2015, this was one hospital and now it is 10 hospitals providing state of the art services to patients from all over Pakistan. Now present not only in Karachi but in Sukkur, Larkana, Khairpur, Sehwan, Nawabshah, Hyderabad, Tando Mohammad Khan and even in Mithi (Thar). All facilities including Angiography, Angioplasty, Bypass Heart Surgery, Laboratory, Radiology, Outpatient Consultation, Hospitalization, Medicines are provided TOTALLY FREE OF COST and thousands of patients are treated in these hospitals every day. We also have a unique program of Chest Pain Units, which provide acute management of heart attack and 24 such units are established at different places under flyovers or at hospitals / clinics across the province of Sindh. In 2022, NICVD Health Care Network catered over 2.2 Million patients in its all hospitals and Chest Pain Units. For us to succeed, we need concerted efforts from organization like yours. Since its inception in 1963, the National Institute of Cardiovascular Diseases (NICVD) has played a pivotal role in caring for patients with heart disease in Pakistan.
NICVD is the first tertiary cardiac care institute in South Asia as well as the flagship facility for cardiology in Pakistan with a focus on superior care for patients.
Over 2.3 Million patients are being treated every year in whole system of NICVD Network, Totally free of cost
